## Project Overview  

MeTube is a streamlined, open-source platform inspired by YouTube, designed for a minimalistic and distraction-free viewing experience. The platform focuses on simplicity and ease of use, allowing users to search for and watch videos without unnecessary clutter. Upon opening a video, users have access to just two buttons‚Äîone to view the video‚Äôs description and the other to see the pinned comment‚Äîensuring a clear and straightforward interface. The project is community-driven, encouraging contributions to enhance and maintain the platform's functionality and user experience.
